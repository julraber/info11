After humanity's extinction, the machines did not inherit the Earth; they pierced downward into the burning heart of Hell itself. Driven by an endless hunger for blood, they tore through ancient layers of sin, ruin, and divine punishment, where forgotten armies still scream and dead gods stir beneath mountains of ash. In ULTRAKILL, every descent is a declaration of war, every chamber becomes an altar of violence, and every step forward is carved through demons, angels, and the shattered remains of creation. This is not survival. This is conquest written in steel, fire, and holy wrath.
